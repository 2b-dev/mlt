About Us

ASEC Frontier, founded in 2017, is a company led by Prof. Dr. Thapana Boonlar as a Chief Executive Officer. The company focuses on the development of medical platform which dedicates to deliver the latest medical information including news and academic researches for medical professionals.

CEO greetings

I am sincerely grateful for the support toward our ‘Medical Leaders Thailand’ application. Our company, ASEC Frontier (Thailand) Co., Ltd., determines to administrate the business transparently and beneficially for Thai society, especially to promote education and medical issue. Our company starts to develop ‘Medical Leaders Thailand’ to be a hub of medical information across the world that up-to-date and reliable for our customers. we hope that in the future, ‘Medical Leaders Thailand’ will alleviate medical issue gap in Thailand. Customers can get medical information conveniently, quickly, and effectively based on our reliable resources such as BMJ, JAMA, The Lancet, Care Net, etc. We are also the only company in Thailand that partners up with the biggest medical news site in Japan ‘Medical Tribune’. We will continue to put all of our efforts to serve our customer needs by providing reliability medical information with high quality and support. We wish for the health and happiness to all customers.
